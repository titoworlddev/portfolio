About me

Hello my name is Cristian Arias.

I'm from Spain, I'm 30 years old and I'm a mobile application developer, specifically I use Dart and Flutter, I'm also a web developer, with knowledge in HTML, CSS, JS. I studied mechanics and it was my main profession, but when the Covid pandemic arrived, it awoke in me the desire that I always had to learn programming and I decided to take courses and tutorials online.
